Mythical Creatures Griffin 1/4 oz Gold Coin
Details Minted from California Gold Rush bullion, the $20 Liberty Double Eagle displays Liberty with a coronet and a heraldic eagle.
Specifications Designer(s): James B. Longacre
Gross Weight: 33.44 g
Composition: 90% Gold, 10% Copper
Content: 0.9675 oz
Date(s): 1849–1907
Condition: BU, MS-64
IRA Approved: No
Coin Highlights Multiples of 20 are packaged in plastic tubes. All other coins will be in protective packaging.
Obverse: Shows Liberty, facing left, encircled by 13 stars with the word “Liberty” on her hair band.
Reverse: Features a heraldic eagle with a shield on its breast with thirteen stars above. Surrounding the eagle is “United States of America” and the denomination.
U.S. Mint issue from the following mints: Philadelphia, Carson City, Denver, New Orleans or San Francisco.
